❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start farming aura?

Simply use the /start command in any channel where Aurix is active. The more you interact, the more aura you accumulate!

Can I use Aurix on my own server?

Yes! Click the "Add Bot to Your Server" button and authorize Aurix. You'll have full access to all features.

What are the rewards for ranking up?

Higher ranks unlock exclusive roles, badges, bonus aura multipliers, and access to premium competitions.

Is Aurix free to use?

Yes, Aurix is completely free! All core features are available without any paywalls.

How can I report bugs or suggest features?

Join our official Discord server and use the #suggestions and #bug-reports channels. We read and respond to all feedback!
